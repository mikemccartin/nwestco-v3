Nwestco Announces Acquisition of Arkoma Services, LLC

Nwestco, LLC has completed its acquisition of Arkoma Services, LLC, effective March 29, 2024. Based in Fort Smith, Arkansas, Arkoma brings over 25 years of expertise in petroleum compliance, regulatory services, calibration, testing, and fuel polishing.

Leadership & Operations

Mike will continue leading the Arkoma branch to ensure service continuity. The company maintains its Fort Smith facility while gaining access to Nwestco's inventory and support network.

Strategic Value

"The integration of Arkoma Services into the Nwestco family represents a significant step forward in our growth within the Arkansas market."
— Tim Gibbar, CEO, Nwestco

Market Expansion

This acquisition complements Nwestco's 2019 acquisition of Hooten Equipment in Springdale, Arkansas, enabling broader petroleum installation and service capabilities across the region. The deal strengthens Nwestco's presence in Arkansas, Oklahoma, and Missouri.

Nwestco operates from Denver with offices across the Pacific Northwest, Arkansas, and California, functioning as a portfolio company of Guardian Capital Partners.
