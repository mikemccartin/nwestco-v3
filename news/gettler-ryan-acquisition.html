Nwestco Acquires Gettler-Ryan Inc.

Nwestco, a Guardian Capital Partners portfolio company, has acquired Gettler-Ryan Inc., a petroleum services provider based in Dublin and Sacramento, California. The deal expands Nwestco's Northern California operations.

What They Do

Gettler-Ryan specializes in turnkey petroleum services to the retail and industrial end-markets, enhancing Nwestco's existing service capabilities in the region.

Leadership Perspective

"This partnership represents a shared commitment to excellence, partnership, and service."
— Tim Gibbar, CEO, Nwestco
"This acquisition allows us to enhance the scale and scope of services provided, while continuing to meet the customers needs at the highest level of quality."
— Ryan May, COO, Nwestco

About Nwestco

The company operates 15 branch locations across the Western and Midwest U.S., employing over 250 field technicians with 30+ years of combined industry experience in fuel and car wash equipment maintenance and compliance services.
